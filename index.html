About Us

We are a student-led project aiming to provide underprivileged students with technology through donations and fundraisers.

About Our Project

What We Do.

ProjectCROS is a Non-Profit organization founded by High School students attending Dougherty Valley High School located in the Bay Area, who are passionate about technology and helping less fortunate communities. We are targeting schools in the Bay Area that do not have the funding necessary to purchase enough laptops. We also plan to collect computers from communities willing to donate as well as purchase brand new laptops. Then we will refurbish the donated computers and give them to low-income schools.On top of this, we would like to introduce the basic concepts of programming, web design/app development, video editing, photoshop, and more.
